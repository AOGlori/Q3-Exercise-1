Justice League(2001)

The Justice League is a animated series that follows the adventures of the heros that would fight crime to the world

Their members consists of:Superman, Wonder Woman, Batman(part-timer),Green Lantern(John Stewart),Hawkgirl,and Martian Manhunter(J'onn J'onzz).

Wonder Woman(also known as Diana Prince) is one of the founding members of the Justice League. She is raised by her mother, Queen Hippolyita of Themyscira. She is also the princess of the amazons.

Batman(aka Bruce Wayne) is one of the Justice League members which in this series he said that he is going to be a part-timer member of the League although he still fights crime along side them. He resides in Gotham city.

Superman(aka Clark Kent or Kal-El) was born on a planet called Krypton but it was destroyed during an explosion. He is also a member of the Justice League. He resides in Smallvile and Metropolis.
